NIO is the creator behind the Inside Your Library suite — a collection of self-discovery apps built on the belief that the most important things about a person are already written into them, waiting to be read.

Each app in the library draws on a different ancient tradition — palmistry, dream science, iridology, graphology, numerology, astrology — and makes it accessible, personal and precise for the modern reader.
