THE LIBRARIAN

About Louise

I am Louise. I tend the library.

Inside Your Library is a quiet place. The books and apps here carry every kind of human knowledge — palmistry, dreams, iridology, handwriting, numerology, natural remedies, interactive children's stories, faith stories, and more to come. The volumes have always been here, kept on these shelves by librarians long before me. My role is simply to keep them, and to help you find what you came for.

The belief that holds this place together is this: the most important things about you have already been written. The reading of them is older than any of us. The library is where that reading is kept.

New wings open as new collections arrive. There is no rush. The books are patient.
